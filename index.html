About Our School

हमारे स्कूल के बारे में

Established in 1942, M F Group of Schools is a recognized minority institution providing quality education from Play School to Class 8. We offer both English and Hindi medium instruction with modern facilities. Founded by Syed Aziz Madany, the entity later transitioned to leadership under Syed Najmuddin Ahmad Madany, who served as chairman of the trust and lifetime member. His role extended to consultancy services across affiliated NGOs, including the M F Society, trust initiatives, and related organizations, overseeing strategic guidance and operational frameworks.

  • Minority Status (Govt. Recognized)
  • Affordable fee structure
  • Special concessions for BPL families
  • Experienced teaching staff
  • Safe and nurturing environment
